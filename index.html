Treasure Collector!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game

There are four sea creatures below. By clicking on a creature you will be adding a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number, you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

The value of the creatures is hidden from you until you click on them.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the value of the creatures!
